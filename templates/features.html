So what can MapMe do for you?


1. Always know where your infomation is headed

Don't be surprised when you find out you're submitting personal information to some crazy country you've never heard of before.


2. Find out where sites are hosted before you visit

Did you receive a suspicious link that's worth investigating before visiting? Lookup the site location with MapMe before visiting so you don't risk leaking out personal information.



3. See a feed of recent website lookups

Maybe you're just a data geek and want to see what countries have sites that have been checked recently. That's cool with us, we don't judge. We also don't keep a record of who is checking these sites, so don't worry about your information being public.
